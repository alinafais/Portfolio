About Me

UX Designer and Frontend Web Developer

As a UX Design and Research Specialist and a Web Developer, I bring a strong foundation in user-centered design principles, gained through my graduate studies at the University of Michigan and hands-on experience with diverse projects. I have led usability testing sessions, studies and developed high-fidelity prototypes and wireframes using Figma and Adobe XD. My work has resulted in significant improvements in design functionality, task completion rates, and user satisfaction. With expertise in thematic analysis, heuristic evaluations, and A/B testing, I excel in synthesizing user research insights to create intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling digital solutions that prioritize user needs and enhance engagement. I have applied these skills to various projects, leading user testing, creating high-fidelity prototypes, and refining digital interfaces to enhance user satisfaction and engagement. Driven by a passion for understanding user behavior and needs, I employ a range of qualitative research methods, including interviews, focus groups, user personas, surveys, and usability testing, to inform my design process.
