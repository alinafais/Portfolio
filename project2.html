Nevada Homeless Alliance Mobile App Case Study

Your Path to Essential Resources, Simplified

Objective

The objective of NHA Mobile App is to design and develop a user-centered mobile application for the Nevada Homeless Alliance (NHA) that enhances accessibility and usability for individuals experiencing homelessness or at risk of eviction. The app seeks to address critical challenges such as limited internet access, varying levels of tech literacy, and the lack of a unified platform for essential resources. By providing personalized resource recommendations for shelters, healthcare, food assistance, and other vital services, the application aims to empower users to connect with timely and reliable support. The app focuses on creating an intuitive and inclusive design that ensures a seamless experience, fostering independence and improving the quality of life for underserved populations.

Nevada's Homelessness and Accessibility Challenges

Nevada is grappling with a worsening crisis where homelessness and accessibility challenges converge. Nearly 47.3% of the homeless population has disabilities, emphasizing the urgent need for accessible services. Additionally, 55% of homeless individuals in the state are unsheltered, leaving them without access to vital resources. This group faces heightened health risks due to barriers in receiving care, while chronic homelessness, which has risen by 30%, disproportionately impacts individuals with disabilities. These statistics underscore the pressing need for inclusive solutions to address the needs of Nevada’s most vulnerable populations.

Key Features

  • Food Assistance: Provides access to hot meals, food banks, and meal pantries for individuals in need.
  • Shelter and Housing Support: Includes temporary shelters, housing assistance, and coordinated entry for stable accommodations.
  • Medical and Mental Health Services: Offers medical and dental services, mental health support, and detox resources for individuals requiring care.
  • Financial Guidance: Provides financial management tools and resources, including donation centers and support for monetary stability.
  • Hygiene Services: Includes access to showers, laundry facilities, and hygiene kits for individuals.
  • Transportation Assistance: Provides resources like bus tickets and shuttle services to improve mobility.
  • Legal Aid and Documentation Support: Assists with government documents, legal issues, and birth certificates for those in need.
  • Job and Workforce Development: Connects individuals to job staffing agencies, employment opportunities, and workforce services.
  • Support for Vulnerable Groups: Includes services tailored to women, LGBTQIA+, veterans, seniors, and teenagers.
  • Community Resources: Access to libraries, recreation centers, and resource hubs for education and social engagement.

Competitive Data Analysis

The charts illustrate the proportion of individuals and families experiencing chronic homelessness in the United States, highlighting the disparity between sheltered and unsheltered populations. Although women represent a smaller portion of the overall homeless population, homelessness among women has been steadily increasing. Since 2022, homelessness among women has risen by 12.1%, and by 11.4% since 2015, with the growth being particularly notable among individual women. Despite fewer women experiencing homelessness compared to men, this trend is deeply concerning. A significant factor is that women are more vulnerable to harassment and assault, with living outdoors further heightening this risk. Additionally, the unsheltered population has nearly doubled between 2022 and 2023. On a national scale, the number of homeless families has also been on the rise since 2020.

Wireframes

Following the initial sketching phase, I created wireframes using Figma to further refine the design of the NHA Mobile App. These wireframes serve as a blueprint, outlining the structure and layout of the app's key screens, including navigation, resource directories, and user interaction elements. The wireframes focus on usability, ensuring that the app is intuitive and accessible to the target audience. While these wireframes represent my initial vision, they will be iterated upon collaboratively with the team to ensure the final design meets all user needs and project goals.
